Refund and Cancellation Policy

Important: Please read this policy carefully before making any purchase on Phantrics platform.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available under the following circumstances:

  • Service not delivered within the promised timeframe
  • Service significantly differs from the description provided
  • Technical issues on our end prevent service delivery
  • Duplicate payment charges

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the transaction date.

2. Non-Refundable Services

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Services already delivered or completed
  • Customized or personalized services once work has commenced
  • Services used or partially consumed
  • Change of mind after service delivery

3. Cancellation Policy

Users can cancel services under these conditions:

  • Before Service Initiation: Full refund within 24 hours of purchase
  • After Service Initiation: Cancellation allowed but refund subject to review
  • Partial Delivery: Partial refund may be provided based on completion percentage

4. How to Request Refund

To request a refund or cancellation:

  1. Send an email to support.phantrics@gmail.com
  2. Include your transaction ID and order details
  3. Provide reason for refund request
  4. Attach relevant proof or screenshots if applicable

5. Refund Processing Timeline

Review Period: 2-3 business days to review your request

Approval Notification: You will be notified via email once approved

Processing Time: 5-7 business days after approval

Bank Credit: Additional 2-3 days depending on your bank

Total Timeline: 10-14 business days from request to account credit

6. Refund Method

Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used for the transaction:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refund to the same card
  • UPI: Refund to the same UPI ID
  • Net Banking: Refund to the same bank account
  • Wallet: Refund to the same wallet

7. Partial Refunds

In certain cases, partial refunds may be offered:

  • When service is partially delivered
  • When resources have already been allocated
  • When cancellation occurs mid-service

The refund amount will be calculated based on the work completed and resources utilized.

8. Failed or Declined Transactions

If payment is deducted but transaction fails:

  • Amount will be automatically refunded within 5-7 business days
  • If not received, contact us with transaction details
  • We will coordinate with payment gateway for resolution

9. Contact for Refunds

Email: support.phantrics@gmail.com

Response Time: 24-48 hours

Business Hours: Monday - Saturday, 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM IST

10. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this Refund and Cancellation Policy. Changes will be communicated via email or platform notification.
