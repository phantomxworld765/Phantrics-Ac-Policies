Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 01, 2026

This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines the terms under which refunds and cancellations are processed for our services.

1. Cancellation Policy

1.1 Customer Cancellation

Customers may request to cancel an order within 24 hours of placing it. The cancellation terms are as follows:

Within 24 Hours: If the cancellation request is made within 24 hours of placing the order, a full refund will be processed and credited to the source account within 7 working days.

After 24 Hours: If the cancellation request is made after 24 hours, 10% of the total order value will be forfeited as a cancellation fee. The remaining amount will be refunded within 7 working days.

No cancellation requests will be entertained once the order has been processed for dispatch or delivery.

1.2 Cancellation by Us

We reserve the right to cancel any order in the following circumstances:

  • Product or service is unavailable
  • Pricing or product information errors
  • Fraudulent or suspicious transactions
  • Force majeure events

In such cases, a full refund will be processed within 7 working days.

2. Refund Policy

2.1 Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be requested in the following situations:

  • Product or service not delivered as described
  • Technical issues preventing service usage
  • Duplicate payment or billing errors
  • Cancellation within the allowed timeframe

2.2 Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact us at phantrics@gmail.com within 7 days of the transaction. Include the following information:

  • Order ID or transaction reference
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for refund request
  • Supporting documentation (if applicable)

2.3 Refund Timeline

Once your refund request is received and approved:

  • Refund will be processed within 5-7 working days
  • The amount will be credited to your original payment method
  • Bank processing time may vary (typically 5-10 business days)

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following items/services are non-refundable:

  • Digital products that have been downloaded or accessed
  • Customized or personalized services
  • Services already rendered or completed
  • Promotional or discounted items (unless defective)

4. Payment Failures

In case of payment failures or transaction errors, the amount will be automatically refunded to your account within 7-10 working days by your bank or payment gateway.

5. Contact Us

For any queries related to refunds or cancellations, please contact us at:

Email: phantrics@gmail.com

We typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days.
